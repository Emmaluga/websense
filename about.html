Mission statement

Front end development is not just the user interactivity aspect of a website, it is taking the vision and design concept from the client and implementing it through code.

My mission statement is making websites so simple that, there are obviously no deficiences. From having a creative eye on images and how to structure them, to understanding color theory and how it affects the user's mood, and still, aligning all these concepts to the clients brand, front end development becomes crucial to the success of displaying information to the targeted users.

This is my aim, this is my mission statement in the creative space of web development.
